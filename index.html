About Errant

Errant turns everyday activities into real-world quests. Complete challenges in Discovery, Wellness, Social, Creativity, and Adventure to earn XP, build streaks, climb the leaderboard, and share your journey with friends.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I earn XP?

Complete quests from the Quest Board, take on the Quest of the Week for bonus rewards, and challenge friends. Maintain a daily streak to unlock XP multipliers up to 4x.

What are ranks and leagues?

As you earn XP you progress through ranks — Scout, Knight, and Champion — and compete in Bronze, Silver, and Gold leagues on the leaderboard.

How do challenges work?

Challenge a friend to complete a quest before time runs out. The winner takes the XP stake. You can view active challenges in the Friends tab.

Is my data private?

Your feed posts can be shared publicly or limited to friends only. We do not sell your personal data. For full details, please contact us using the link below.
